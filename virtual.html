Gallery 143 is excited to annouce our newest opportunity.....virtual shopping!

You are now able to shop from the comfort of your home. Using the Zoom platform, the ladies at Gallery 143 will welcome you to the store virtually, walk you through the shop using an electronic device, help you pick out the perfect gift or addition for your home, package it for either curbside pick up or shipping. For a nominal fee, Gallery 143 will even gift wrap your purchase and include an appropriate one-of-a-kind card for curbside pick up or delivery to you or gift recipient

What do you need to experience this new and innovative way of shoping at Gallery 143?

  • Zoom downloaded on your device
  • Make a virtual shopping appointment
  • Make a cup of tea and enjoy!
