Meet the staff at Gallery 143.

Linda

Fiber artist and custom framer

Green, Ohio

As a fine arts major in collage, Linda was immediately drawn to fiber arts. Over the years, her passion for working with fibers has remained with her as well as adding newly discovered types of fiber art. Along with being a mother as well as once owning her own shop, she has enjoyed being a custom framer for the past 25 years.

Bev

Artist

Green, Ohio

A lifelong resident of the Green area, Bev has always found a love of arts and crafts. She has enjoyed completing many works of art throughout her life, most of them primarily fiber art.

Ellen

Jewelry artist

Portage Lakes, Ohio

Ellen was very young when she fell in love with jewelry; pursuing her first metal smithing class at the age of 13. Since then, she has sold her work in art galleries, custom jewelry stores, at arts festivals, and through internet sales. Ellen finds inspiration in many places, including her environment, friends, and shiny treasures she sees and......often wants.
